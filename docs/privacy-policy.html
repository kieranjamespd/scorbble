Privacy Policy

Scorbble — Scrabble Score Keeper

Last updated: December 9, 2024

Summary: Scorbble does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Information We Collect

Scorbble does not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely offline and stores all data locally on your device.

Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Player names you enter
  • Game scores and history
  • Player profiles and statistics

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Scorbble does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Internet Connection

Scorbble works completely offline. The app does not require or use an internet connection.

Children's Privacy

Scorbble does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear All Games" option in the app
  • Deleting the app from your device

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
